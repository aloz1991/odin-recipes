The Perfect Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich in a nonstick pan with buttered bread and American Cheddar for a classic hot sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter, divided
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat skiller over medium heat
  2. Place butter in skiller, place slice of bread and add cheese
  3. Cook until lightly browned
