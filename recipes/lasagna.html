Delicious Lasagna Recipe

This Lasagna recipe takes a little bit of work, but it's so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Sugar
  • Garlic
  • Noodles
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Sautee the onions
  2. Bake the sugar
  3. Sautee the garlic, then add to the sauteed onions
  4. Boil the noodles in water until tender
  5. Whisk eggs into the garlic and onion mixture
